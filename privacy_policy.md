# Privacy Policy

TzolkinWaves does not collect or share any personal information. It does not use ads, in-app-payments or similar.

It is free as in "free beer" and as in "free speech".

You don't have to trust anybody, it is open source under the MIT license, the source code of the app can be found at [GitHub](https://github.com/Release-Candidate/TzolkinWaves).
